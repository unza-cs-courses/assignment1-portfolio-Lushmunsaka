About Me

Hello! I'm Lushomo Munsaka, a passionate web developer with a keen eye for design and a love for creating intuitive, dynamic user experiences. With over five years of experience in the tech industry, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and enterprise clients to bring their digital visions to life. My journey into web development began during my university studies in Computer Science, where I discovered my fascination with the intersection of technology and creativity. What started as a curiosity quickly evolved into a career passion. I thrive on solving complex problems and turning ideas into functional, beautiful websites and applications. I actively contribute to open-source projects in my local tech community. My goal is to build digital solutions that not only meet business objectives but also delight users and make a positive impact. When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me playing fighting games and brushing up on my combos or some other video game, watching anime, or diving into a visual novel. I believe that these diverse experiences fuel my creativity, and allows me to bring this perspective to every project I undertake.
